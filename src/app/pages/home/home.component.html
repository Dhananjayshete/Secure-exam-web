How It Works

From setup to submission in three easy steps.

01

Setup & Scheduling

Teachers create exams using our rich text editor or import from question banks. Set strict timings, randomization rules, and assign to specific student batches instantly.

02

Secure Authentication

Students log in and undergo a 3-step verification process: ID scan, face match, and environment check to ensure the room is clear of unauthorized materials.

03

The Examination

The exam begins in full-screen mode. Autosave protects answers every 30 seconds. If the internet connection drops, students can resume exactly where they left off.
